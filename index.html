Nashra Flour Mill Uppala has been a trusted name in Uppala, Kerala since 2015. We blend time-honored milling techniques with modern standards to deliver the freshest, most aromatic flours and spices to your kitchen.

Our commitment to quality begins with hand-selecting the finest raw materials from trusted suppliers. Every product that leaves our mill meets the highest standards of purity and taste.

Our Process

From Source to Your Kitchen

Every step is carefully executed to deliver unmatched quality

01

Selection

We hand-select the finest raw materials from trusted local suppliers to ensure premium quality.

02

Cleaning

Thorough multi-stage cleaning removes all impurities, guaranteeing purity in every batch.

03

Drying

Materials are dried to optimal moisture for perfect grinding consistency and longer shelf life.
